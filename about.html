About the club

West Norfolk Lawn Tennis club is located in North Runcton, a village three miles outside of Kings Lynn. The club was founded in 1951, making it one of the oldest tennis clubs in the area.
The club boasts three beautiful grass courts that are available for use from April to September and two hard courts, allowing for year-round tennis.
Twice weekly club nights are held throughout the summer where members can expect to get lots of tennis action. There are also closed and open tournaments held at the club throughout the year.
For those looking for a more competitive edge the club has several teams that play in the local tennis league and is always welcoming more players.
The club welcomes tennis players of all ages and abilities so rest assured you will fit right in with this friendly club.
