Grass Courts

There are three beautiful grasscourts available for use from April to September. The courts are well maintained giving players a great grass experience. Come and join us for a game and you could be mistaken for thinking you're playing on centre court at Wimbledon!

Hard Courts

There are two hard courts available for use year-round. These give a good, consistant playing surface where the ball bounces predictably.
